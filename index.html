About Me

I’m a 3D visualizer with a Master’s degree in Architecture and a strong background in design, 3D modeling, and rendering. I specialize in creating high-quality interior and exterior visualizations for a wide range of building types, including residential, commercial, and public spaces. My work spans various architectural styles, from clean and minimal Nordic aesthetics to timeless Classic and refined Neo-Classical designs. I pay close attention to detail, atmosphere, and light to bring each concept to life and help clients visualize their projects with clarity and emotion.
